Latest projects

Safego Mobile Application

Safego is a mobile application designed to ensure the safety of students traveling to and from school. It provides real-time tracking of school buses, updates on arrival times, and emergency notifications to parents and school authorities.

Blood Donation Mobile Application

This mobile application facilitates blood donation by connecting donors with nearby blood banks and donation drives. It features a dashboard that displays the current inventory of blood packets and the percentage of available blood types, helping users to easily find where their donation is needed most.

Foody Mobile Application

Foody is a food delivery application that connects users with a wide range of local restaurants. It offers an intuitive user interface for browsing menus, placing orders, and tracking deliveries in real-time, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable food ordering experience.

Lanka Pay Desktop Application

Lanka Pay is a desktop banking application offering essential features such as user login, account signup, deposits, cash withdrawals, PIN changes, mini bank statements, and quick cash options. Designed to provide a secure and convenient banking experience from the comfort of your home or office.
